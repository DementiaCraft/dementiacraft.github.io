What is DementiaCraft?

DementiaCraft is a community, started by some older, more seasoned players who wanted to create a server where players, all aged 25 and above, could share a passion for crafting, building, and exploring this pixelated world. Our mission is to cultivate a virtual haven, free from toxicity, and brimming with respect, responsibility, and fun.

DementiaCraft is a safe space and we expect nothing less than kindness, inclusivity, and maturity – that means we vet people before whitelisting and only accept applications if you are 25+ of age

DementicaCraft is home.

How new is DementiaCraft ?

The gates to Dementia opened on the fourth of August, 2023 and just a few weeks later, the community ventured out together to slay the dragon, armed only with bows and arrows and no armor! The event was a huge success and we are looking forward to many more!

We have established a Shopping District at spawn which is starting to come alive, with community builds like a public spider spawner and a community house, and player owned shops which we highly encourage all to build and take part in the bartering (no plug-ins, honor system) – more on this when you’re invited to the discord.

Features

We play on a semi-vanilla, whitelisted server with no resets* – we have implemented a world border that will grow with the community and Minecraft updates. We also enjoy a live dynmap! *We will be resetting ‘The End’ and parts of ‘The Nether’ quarterly.

Semi-Vanilla?

While we enjoy the vanilla Minecraft experience, we have added a few Quality of Life plug-ins, to make the gaming experience even more enjoyable, some of these are:

  • Double Shulker shells
  • Fast leaf decay
  • 1 player sleep
  • Silence mobs
  • Player head drops, Mobheads, mini-blocks, and statues

Changes to mobs:

  • No phantoms
  • Bats drop membranes
  • We’ve also provided the Endermen with a nice invisible block, so they won’t rearrange your terraforming

Join DementiaCraft

DementiaCraft is considered a safe space and welcomes all players regardless of gender identity, race, religion, etc. No griefing, stealing, bullying, insulting, discrimination of any kind, or prejudiced behavior is tolerated. Toxic behavior is not welcome.

We encourage constructive communication, cooperation, and collaboration, enabling everyone to flourish and develop their skills in a welcoming atmosphere.

Expectations

We are looking for players who are:

  • Kind, respectful, and wanting to hang out and be part of a fun community
  • Community-minded — not focused solely on completing everything solo, but eager to share and build experiences together.
  • Ages 25 and older
  • Not using hacks or cheats, ie. no x-ray, baritone etc.
  • Willing to be active on our discord server

What can you expect from us?

  • All of the above — plus a dedicated and communicative team of founders who are active both in-game and on Discord.
  • A high-performance server built for long-term play.
  • Grief protection to keep your builds and progress safe.

Application process

If you wish to join us, please fill out this survey, then we will reach out to you via discord

By joining our community, you agree to abide by these guidelines. Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from the community at the discretion of the moderators.

Server Rules

DementiaCraft is a server focused on PvE. We expect kindness, maturity, community and common sense from our Geezers (that's you)!

Should you like to have that more defined, here are our go and no-go

GO

  • Be kind
  • Patch creeper holes to the best of your ability
  • Be respectful of players and builds
  • Make farms as lag free as possible - we also have AFK-kick, to maintain a smooth server experience for all, you will be kicked after 3hrs of AFK-time
  • Friendly pranking between friends is allowed, as long as you are prepared to do the cleanup and aren’t destroying stuff. If in doubt, don’t do it
  • Check with new neighbors before setting up base

NO GO

  • Griefing: We don't accept any form of griefing or stealing, don't mess with others stuff like builds (including but not limited to; portals, shops, farms, modes of transport etc.), areas or mobs.
  • PvP: pvp is only okay, if consensual.
  • No bases at spawn or the shopping district.
  • No third party clients / hacking / X-ray or the like, if you have questions about a mod, please check with support.
  • No mods that gives an advantage over regular vanilla minecraft - ie. litematica is allowed, litematica-printing is not - no-clip freecam is allowed, full freecam is not etc.
  • Chunk loaders/systems are not permitted, neither are perimeters without express consent from the Elders.
  • The community at large should have access to all possible limited experiences, please check with the Elders beforehand.
  • SE quadrant of the End is a strictly no-fly zone. This to keep the option to successfully explore and raid vanilla style - see announcements for more on this.
  • No duping of any blocks, only exception is TNT in tree farms and cobblestone farms. If you have questions or requests, do send a DM to the Elders
  • No silverfish mining
  • Please do not strip copper or farm Trial Chambers within the blue border. You may copper strip the trial chambers in the borderlands (the area between the soft and hard border)

Shopping District

Welcome to the Shopping District

To make sure we keep a dynamic and buzzing shopping district, we have the following rules:

  • No bases at spawn or in/under/over/around the shopping district.
  • All builds must be an active shop (beautifying areas are welcome).
  • New shops must be built in the currently active space.
  • Shops are run on a bartering honor system: shops decide their chosen payment, and payment must be placed when you pick up your purchase (ie: 1 stack of logs = 2 diamonds. Replace 1 stack of logs with your 2 diamonds).

Everyone is allowed to establish a shop in the shopping district following these steps:

Buy a plot of available space on the currently active island

  • Plots are 10 by 10 blocks and cost 1 diamond block to purchase
  • You may purchase 5 plots per shop build
  • The plots must be connected (20×20 square? – 10×30 rectangle? – L-shaped? – T-shaped?)
  • You may only make 1 shop per connected plot purchase

Your shop must have a minor and themed assortment of inventory (no malls)

  • Keep inventory themed, ie: logs // stones // wool // books // gunpowder and fireworks // etc.
  • Ask the Elders if in doubt
  • The build doesn’t have to match your inventory, but you’re welcome to theme that too

You must hook up the shop to a path / establish a path

You must announce your shop

  • In the #ShoppingDistrict channel
  • Mark it on the in-game map
  • The shop must have 1 visible sign stating the ownership

No price gouging

  • price your inventory reasonably and competitively, and do not undercut other businesses
